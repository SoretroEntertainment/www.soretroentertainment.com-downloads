Jesse Jackson's Debut Album
Jesse Jackson is a revolutionary artist who originates from New York City. Born in 2029, he was growing up adopted in the city's slums,
he used to rule a small gang to prey on wall street yuppies. With so much violence he witnessed, he found himself by making music. His main inspiration was Devon Hendryx and Kanye West.
Before his debut album, Jesse Jackson put out a song "Bloat" and sold it out through selling Zyme. He self-released it on Bandcamp for free, then the song got on Spotify and iTunes. It was a hit for the entire East Coast.
After he made millions on this song, he and Kurt Kingston recorded an album together. Kurt didn't ask for the credit so it's all about Jesse Jackson, which is the point of this album.
The self-titled album was self-published in 2052, on Bandcamp for 33.33$, on Spotify and iTunes. But not on SoundCloud. Kurt claimed that SoundCloud is "a platform for bums".
Okay but in all seriousness, it's a huge mix of my favourite songs, yes even the wind SFX.
